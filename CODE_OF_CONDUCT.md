# Code of Conduct

Hyred is a small open-source project. It only works if people feel welcome to contribute. This document sets the basics.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make the Hyred community a friendly, professional space for everyone — regardless of experience level, identity, background, or personal characteristics. We want this to be the kind of project people recommend to friends starting their first PR.

## Expected behaviour

- Be kind, patient, and assume good faith
- Give and accept feedback gracefully — review code, not people
- Stay focused on what's best for the project and its users
- Credit others' contributions
- Help newcomers find their footing

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained hostility
- Harassment in any form, including unwanted contact or attention
- Sharing someone's private information without permission
- Trolling, deliberate intimidation, or disruption
- Any behaviour a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces — the GitHub repo, Discussions, Issues, PRs, and any official channel where someone is acting on behalf of the project. It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you see or experience behaviour that violates this Code, please report it privately to the maintainer at **hello@hyred.app**.

When you report, please include:

- What happened (links to comments/PRs/messages if applicable)
- When it happened
- Anyone else involved or affected
- Whether you'd like to be kept anonymous in any response

Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially. We won't share your identity with the reported party without your consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, including:

1. A private conversation and a request to change course
2. A public warning in the relevant thread
3. A temporary cooldown from project spaces
4. A permanent ban from the project

We aim for the lightest action that resolves the problem and protects the community.
